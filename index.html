About Me

Hello! My name is Matthew Holzer and I am an aspiering Software Engineer and Video Game Developer.

I am currently a student at The College of New Jersey majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Digital and Creative Technology. I am involved in many clubs and activities on campus, such as Game Design Club, Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), Digit.all, TCNJ's Musical Theater of which I am the Lead Carpenter, and I am the president of TCNJ's chapter of the Kappa Theta Pi Professional Technology Fraternity.

Projects

Where The Moon Meets The Sun

Independent project developing a tactical role-playing game using Unity. Inspired by games such as Fire Emblem and Final Fantasy Tactics, this game has the player strategically maneuver their units to defeat opposing enemy units. Each map presents different objectives, such as routing all enemies, defeating the boss unit, or safely moving all units to the escape point.
