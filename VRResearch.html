VR Research Project

Researching how VR can be used for sports training purposes

GITHUB

Overview

For this research project, I am exploring the application of camera-based motion capture and Virtual Reality in sports training. Numerous studies have demonstrated that training with someone who shares the same height, gender, and skin color can enhance performance. Our hypothesis is that a virtual reality simulation, where users can see their motions replicated by a model with similar physical attributes, can further improve performance and accessibility in sports training.

To achieve this, we are utilizing Rokoko Studios to capture motion data, which will then be applied to rigs obtained from Mixamo. The development of the simulation will be carried out using Unity and Virtual Reality technology, enabling users to visualize their movements and assess their performance. This innovative approach aims to create a more personalized and effective training experience.
